Frakes Bot for Slack

Wanna get interrupted by Jonathon Frakes? Simply enter an /interruptme slash command.

Install it on Your Slack Team

Setting up Frakes Bot in Slack is simple. Just click the button to log in to your Slack account and choose a team!

Once you install the bot, you should be able to use the slash command on your team chat room. Just type /interruptme.

Developed by Vanessa Archambault (@zoombear) with Node.js and a ton of help from this tutorial.
